Welcome!

Introduction to Technical Communication is an online learning course on technical writing and communication strategies.

What is technical communication?

The Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers Professional Communication Society (IEEE PCS 2007) explains that technical communication:

"includes the study, development, improvement, and promotion of effective techniques for preparing, organizing, processing, editing, collecting, conserving, teaching, and disseminating any form of technical information by and to individuals and groups by any method of communication."

This course aims to introduce you to the world of technical communication and to provide you with the necessary tools to understand the fundamentals and progress further in this field.

About this course.

At the end of this course, you'll be able to:

  • discuss, in your own words, what is meant by technical terms relating to audience analysis, writing style, summaries, typography and colour and visuals
  • explain, in writing, the main ideas taught in each lesson through paragraph-style answers in the assignments provided
  • compare and contrast your own work with that of other students in an online discussion forum
  • defend and critique your writing and design choices using material from the module
  • synthesize information learned in multiple choice questions at the end of each lesson and in a final course assessment
