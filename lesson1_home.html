Lesson 1: Audience Analysis

Audience Analysis

Audience analysis involves assessing the audience to make sure the information provided to them is at the appropriate level. It is one of the first steps in the process of technical writing.

Lesson Objectives

At the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • define an audience
  • investigate what readers ask about documents
  • analyse the communication context
  • gather audience information
  • profile an audience
